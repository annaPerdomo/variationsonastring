04 — About

One person,
many strings.

I'm Anna — a Senior Software Engineer based in Southern California with 8+ years building front-end-heavy SaaS applications. I specialize in React, TypeScript, and Material UI, and I love turning complex UX requirements into pixel-perfect, accessible interfaces. I've shipped features at enrollment-tech company Encoura, built direct mail campaign tools at Nextradata, and got my start as a full-stack engineer at JobFit.

Outside the terminal, I'm a maker in every sense. Crochet, knitting, cross-stitch, sewing, nail art — if it involves yarn, thread, or a hook, I'm probably doing it. I also play piano and sing, which means my evenings are split between debugging and playing scales.

"Variations on a String" isn't just a pun — it's a philosophy. Every project, every stitch, every note is a variation on a theme. Different medium, same love for making something from nothing.
