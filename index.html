03 — About

One person,
many strings.

I'm a full-stack developer with 8 years of experience building web applications that are fast, accessible, and genuinely useful. I've worked across the stack — from pixel-perfect UIs to database architecture and cloud infrastructure.

But beyond the terminal, I'm a maker at heart. Whether I'm charting a new crochet pattern, finishing a cross-stitch piece, or working out harmonies on piano, I find that all creative pursuits inform each other. The patience of crafting makes me a better debugger. The logic of code makes me a better pattern writer.

"Variations on a String" isn't just a pun — it's a philosophy. Every project, every stitch, every note is a variation on a theme. Different medium, same love for making something from nothing.
