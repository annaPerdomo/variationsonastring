Personal Project · 2026

Intermezzo

A Chrome extension that gives you gentle reminders to take short wellness breaks — stretch, fix your posture, and check in with your mind — throughout the workday. Smart stretch selection from a library of 35+ exercises adapts to the time of day, with a built-in timer, illustrated guides, system notifications that reach you in any app, streak tracking, and optional Discord/Slack nudges to keep a team moving together. Named for the musical interlude between movements.

04 — About

One person,
many strings.

I'm Anna Mendez Perdomo — a Software Development Manager based in Southern California with 8+ years building front-end-heavy SaaS applications. I specialize in React, TypeScript, and Material UI, and I love turning complex UX requirements into pixel-perfect, accessible interfaces. I also speak Japanese at an advanced conversational level — I studied abroad at Nanzan University in Nagoya and earned a B.A. in Japanese, Magna Cum Laude, from Cal State Fullerton.

Outside the terminal, I'm a maker in every sense. Crochet, knitting, cross-stitch, sewing, nail art — if it involves yarn, thread, or a hook, I'm probably doing it. I also play piano and sing.

"Variations on a String" isn't just a pun — it's a philosophy. Every project, every stitch, every note is a variation on a theme.
